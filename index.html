Forest

A forest is a large area densely covered with trees and other plants. Forests are home to a diverse range of animal life and play a crucial role in our planet's ecosystem.

Sunset

Sunset is the daily disappearance of the sun below the horizon due to the Earth's rotation. This transition marks the end of the day, painting the sky with vibrant colors of orange, red, and purple.

Birds

Birds are a group of warm-blooded vertebrates constituting the class Aves, characterised by feathers, toothless beaked jaws, the laying of hard-shelled eggs, a high metabolic rate, a four-chambered heart, and a strong yet lightweight skeleton.
